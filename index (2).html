All About SAKADAB

​SAKADAB is a group of 27 physically challenged individuals living in Davao City with different acquired skills who came together from Our Lady of Victory - Training Center to work towards a common goal of increasing the quality of living and becoming self-reliant. ​SAKADAB is a community of differently-abled individuals. As a community, there are no specific programs nor services which they offer. Instead, they provide each other spiritual, moral, and financial support to live a more meaningful life. Some of the activities of the community are farming, baking, handicraft, woodcraft, and welding. The community produces consumer products such as handicrafts.


SAKADAB Profile

What led to the creation of this institution was to provide differently-abled individuals a community where they can provide each other with spiritual, moral and financial support so that they could live a much more meaningful life. These talented people have high hopes and big goals such as living a more meaningful life and being self-reliant. The people in the community produce various consumer products (handicrafts) which can be a source of income especially during these tough times as we are currently experiencing a pandemic. The community also has activities such as farming, baking, etc.
